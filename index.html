Our Story

What started as a humble beginning in 2007 by Harishbhai (Jahangirpura Wala) has now grown into Surat's favorite destination for authentic Aloopuri and Rasawala Khaman.

For almost two decades, we have strictly adhered to our original recipes, using 100% Pure Veg ingredients and home-ground spices to bring that signature sweet, spicy, and tangy burst of flavor to your plate. Today, with 6 locations across Surat, our mission remains the same: to serve fresh, hot, and hygienic Surti street food to our community.
